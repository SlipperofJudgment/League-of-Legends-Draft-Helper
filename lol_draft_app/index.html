          Tag Definitions

          The lanes assigned are mostly based off professional play and reflect the current state of League of Legends as of 6 Aug 2025.

          • Wombo Combo: This champion has great area-of-effect damage or crowd control abilities, or has excellent synergy with champions who possess such abilities.
          • Dive: Self‑explanatory – these champions excel at diving into enemies and initiating fights.
          • Front‑to‑Back: This champion can protect their backline well, focuses on the enemy’s frontline, or acts as a backline carry with strong sustained DPS.
          • Poke: This champion deals great long‑range poke damage that can harass and wear down enemies.
          • Pick: This champion is adept at catching or crowd-controlling an enemy from afar.
